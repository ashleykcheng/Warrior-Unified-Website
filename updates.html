2020-2021 Club Meetings


9.28.2020

Our first virtual club meeting! In this meeting we introduced everyone and talked about our plans for the rest of the year. We did some short stretching and then went into breakout rooms to get to know each other!


10.12.2020

Our second meeting was one fun dance party! Everyone got up and danced to some fun videos. We also did a fun scavenger hunt where students found objects around their room or classroom.


10.26.2020

Our third meeting was Halloween themed! Everyone dressed in costumes in our virtual Halloween party. We danced to some Halloween music videos and then decorated pumpkins together!


11.16.2020

At our fourth meeting we first took our club yearbook photo. Then we did some Thanksgiving activities and drew turkeys together!


12.7.2020

This meeting was Animal themed! We played Guess the Animal, then exercised to a video like our favorite animals!


1.25.2021

Our sixth meeting was Disney themed! We colored Disney characters on a nearpod then exercised like characters from Moana!


2.8.2021

In spirit of Valentines day coming up, this meeting was Valentines Day Themed! Together on a nearpod we shared some of the things we love!


3.1.2021

To celebrate the arrival of Spring, we did a Spring-themed Kahoot together! Afterwards we danced together to a spring dance video.


3.15.2021

At this meeting we celebrated St. Patrick's Day! We followed a nearpod where students drew pictures in response to St. Patrick's Day related prompts.


4.5.2021

This meeting was Superhero themed! We followed a powerpoint and trained together in order to defeat the evil villains!


4.19.2021

Today we learned about animals! We played some fun trivia about animals together!


5.3.2021

Everyone loves Star Wars! We trained from Padawans to Jedis! It was a lot of fun!


5.17.2021

Last Meeting! We said goodbye to everyone and ended off the year!


That's all the meetings for the 2020-2021 school year! Thank you everyone for an amazing year!
