Club Updates


9.28.2020

Our first virtual club meeting! In this meeting we introduced everyone and talked about our plans for the rest of the year. We did some short stretching and then went into breakout rooms to get to know each other!


10.12.2020

Our second meeting was one fun dance party! Everyone got up and danced to some fun videos. We also did a fun scavenger hunt where students found objects around their room or classroom.


10.26.2020

Our third meeting was Halloween themed! Everyone dressed in costumes in our virtual Halloween party. We danced to some Halloween music videos and then decorated pumpkins together!


11.16.2020

At our fourth meeting we first took our club yearbook photo. Then we did some Thanksgiving activities and drew turkeys together!


12.7.2020

This meeting was Animal themed! We played Guess the Animal, then exercised to a video like our favorite animals!


1.25.2021

Our sixth meeting was Disney themed! We colored Disney characters on a nearpod then exercised like characters from Moana!


More Meetings soon!
